📌 Who We Are

At The Silent Elephant, we believe in creating more than just t-shirts — we create wearable identity. Based in Kerala and proudly rooted in quality and creativity, we specialize in custom bulk apparel that helps youth communities, educational institutions, and sports teams bring their brand to life through fashion.

Whether it’s a college fest, inter-department match, startup meet-up, or a university summit, our team ensures every piece of clothing carries the spirit of your event with elegance and energy.

👕 What We Offer

🔹 Product Categories

  • Casual & Dry-fit T-Shirts (Round Neck / V-Neck / Full Sleeve)
  • Sports & Tournament Jerseys (Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Kabaddi, etc.)

🔹 Fabrics Available

  • Dotnet
  • Saleena
  • PP
  • Honeycomb
  • SMK
  • Super Poly

🔹 Printing Techniques

  • DTF Printing – vibrant and detailed print on dark/light fabrics
  • Sublimation – full-body prints for sports jerseys
  • Screen Printing – ideal for simple, bold designs in bulk

🔹 Size Range

S, M, L, XL, XXL, XXXL

📦 How to Place Your Order

  1. Share Your Idea: Send us your concept, logo, or reference designs (even a rough sketch works).
  2. Choose Fabric, Quantity & Sizes: Pick from our material and style options.
  3. Get Pricing & Timeline: We’ll give you a transparent quote and clear delivery date.
  4. Approve & Print: Final designs are shared for approval before printing.
  5. We Deliver to Your Doorstep: Pan-India delivery with trusted courier partners.
